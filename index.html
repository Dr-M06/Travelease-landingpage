Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI trip planning work?

Our AI analyzes your preferences, budget, and travel dates to create personalized itineraries. Simply fill out the trip planner form with your destination interests, and our system will generate custom travel recommendations tailored just for you.

Can I modify my itinerary after it's generated?

Absolutely! Your itinerary is fully customizable. You can add or remove activities, change accommodations, or adjust your schedule at any time. Our AI will help optimize your new plan while maintaining your preferences.

Can I plan trips for groups or families?

Yes! Our platform is perfect for planning group trips. You can specify the number of travelers, including children, and our AI will suggest family-friendly or group-appropriate activities, accommodations, and dining options that everyone will enjoy.

Do you book the trips or just plan them?

With our Basic and Wanderlust packages, we provide detailed planning and recommendations. Our Luxury Voyager package includes a concierge service that can handle bookings on your behalf. For all packages, we provide direct booking links to our trusted travel partners.
